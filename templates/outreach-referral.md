# Referral Outreach Sequence — MCA (3 emails + phone)

> Referral outreach for MCA prospects where a mutual contact has explicitly
> offered an introduction. Referrals close 3–5x faster than cold in the
> MCA industry — speed and credit-sharing with the referrer matter.
> Replace all `[PLACEHOLDER]` fields with prospect-specific info.
> **Compliance:** Never call MCA a "loan." Use *advance*, *capital*, *funding*.

## Pre-Sequence Setup

Before sending Email 1, confirm with `[REFERRER_NAME]`:

1. **Explicit permission** to use their name in the outreach
2. **Context of the referral** — what does the merchant need? (inventory, payroll, equipment, expansion, tax bill)
3. **Ballpark ask** — advance size the merchant mentioned
4. **Heads-up text/email** from the referrer to the merchant:

> **Referrer's template message:**
> *Hey [FIRST_NAME] — I'm connecting you with [YOUR_NAME] at [YOUR_COMPANY].
> They placed my last advance and got me funded in 48 hours with a fair factor.
> Worth a 5-minute call. Expect a note from them today.*

**Critical:** Referrer heads-up raises connect rates from ~15% to ~60%.

## Call 1: The Warm-Intro Call (Day 1)

**Goal:** Reach the merchant within 24 hours while the referrer's endorsement is still fresh.

**Script — merchant answers:**
```
Hey [FIRST_NAME]? This is [YOUR_NAME] at [YOUR_COMPANY] — [REFERRER_NAME] passed
your info along. They said you guys had been talking about [USE_OF_FUNDS /
INITIATIVE] and might want to see some working capital options.

Got 3 minutes? I'll ask you a handful of questions, and if it sounds like there's
a fit, I'll tell you exactly what I need to get you a firm offer by tomorrow.
```

**Pre-qual questions:**
1. How long has [BUSINESS_NAME] been operating?
2. What's monthly gross revenue, ballpark?
3. Any open advances or MCAs right now?
4. What's the capital for?
5. Any tax liens, judgments, or recent bankruptcy?

**Script — voicemail:**
```
Hey [FIRST_NAME], [YOUR_NAME] at [YOUR_COMPANY]. [REFERRER_NAME] asked me to reach
out about working capital for [BUSINESS_NAME]. Shoot me a text or call back at
[YOUR_PHONE] when you get a minute — I can have you a firm offer within
24 hours once we talk. Thanks.
```

## Email 1: The Referral Drop (Day 1)

**Subject Line Options:**
- `[REFERRER_NAME] suggested we connect — working capital for [BUSINESS_NAME]`
- `Intro from [REFERRER_NAME] at [REFERRER_COMPANY]`
- `[REFERRER_NAME] said to reach out — funding for [BUSINESS_NAME]`

**Body:**

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

[REFERRER_NAME] at [REFERRER_COMPANY] gave me your info — said you were looking
at working capital for [BUSINESS_NAME], specifically for [USE_OF_FUNDS_FROM_REFERRER].

Quick context on how we worked with [REFERRER_NAME]:

[BRIEF_REFERRER_STORY — 2 sentences. Example:
"We funded their shop a $75k advance last June to bridge a slow Q3. Factor
came in at 1.29, funded in 48 hours. They've since renewed once at better terms."]

They thought we'd be a fit because [SPECIFIC_REASON — e.g., "you're in the same
industry and dealing with a similar cash-flow rhythm"].

To get you a firm offer within one business day, I just need:

1. **3 months of business bank statements** (all pages, PDF)
2. **Completed 1-page application** (link: [APPLICATION_LINK])
3. **Driver's license** (front)
4. **Voided business check**

Soft inquiry only — no impact on your FICO. No collateral required. If the numbers
don't work, I'll tell you straight.

Happy to grab 10 minutes on the phone first if that's easier:
[CALENDAR_LINK] or just call/text [YOUR_PHONE].

Thanks again to [REFERRER_NAME] for connecting us.

[YOUR_NAME]
[YOUR_TITLE] · [YOUR_COMPANY]
[YOUR_PHONE] · [YOUR_EMAIL]

— All offers subject to underwriting. MCA is a purchase of future receivables, not a loan.
```

**CTA:** Direct — docs or call. Referrals earn the right to a harder ask than cold.
**Timing:** Within 24 hours of the referral. Every day you wait halves your conversion.

## Call 2: Doc-Collection Nudge (Day 3)

**Goal:** Referrals often stall at document submission. One warm call unlocks most of them.

**Script:**
```
Hey [FIRST_NAME] — [YOUR_NAME] again. Just following up to see if the bank
statements came through OK.

[If not:]
Totally get it — everyone hates pulling three months of statements. Two tricks:
 • Most business banking apps let you export them as one PDF in under a minute.
 • If your bookkeeper has them, I can email them directly. Whatever's easier.

What's the fastest way to get them to me today? I'd love to text [REFERRER_NAME]
by end of week and tell them we got you funded.
```

**Social pressure cue:** mentioning the referrer by name is fair game in a doc-nudge — it reminds the merchant someone vouched for them.

> round ([BRIEF_REASON — position/bank health/industry]). No issue, but figured
> you'd want to know. If something changes on their side I'm happy to re-run.*

## Sequence Notes

- **Speed is everything.** Referrals have a half-life of about 72 hours before the merchant loses momentum. Call within 24.
- **Always credit the referrer.** Name them in the subject line, body, and voicemail.
- **Keep the referrer in the loop — always.** Win, lose, or stall, send the update. Referrers who get updates refer again; the ones who are ghosted don't.
- **Pay referral fees promptly.** A paid referrer is a repeat referrer. Spell out the fee structure in your ISO agreement.
- **Do not stack the merchant** without disclosing it to the referrer if there's a relationship risk (e.g., referrer is the merchant's accountant, and stacking would concern them).
- **Never call MCA a "loan."** Use *advance*, *capital*, *funding*, *receivables purchase*.
- **CRM tracking:** tag source as "referral — [REFERRER_NAME]", log fee structure, and set a reminder to thank the referrer whether the deal funds or not.
